Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Entertaining Fights (“we”, “us”) operates entertainingfights.com (“the site”). This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

What the site does

Entertaining Fights is a curated watchlist of combat-sports videos for cardio workouts. You can browse fights without signing in. If you choose to sign in with Google, you can optionally queue fights to a private YouTube playlist on your own YouTube account.

Information we collect

  • Google account information (sign-in only). If you sign in with Google, our authentication provider (Supabase) receives basic profile information from Google, such as your name, email address, and Google account ID. We use this only to identify your account and provide signed-in features.
  • YouTube access (optional). If you sign in with Google and approve YouTube access, we request permission to manage playlists on your YouTube account. We store a Google refresh token and your playlist ID on our servers so we can add or reorder videos in your private playlist when you use “Queue on YouTube.” We do not upload, delete, or modify your videos, comments, or channel settings.
  • Browser storage. Your watched-fight preferences and similar settings may be stored locally in your browser (local storage) on your device.
  • Feedback. If you submit the feedback form, we collect the email address and message you provide so we can read and respond to your note.
  • Technical logs. Our hosting and database providers may automatically record standard technical data (such as IP address, request time, and browser type) when you use the site. We do not use this data to build advertising profiles.

How we use information

We use the information above only to:

  • Operate the site and its fight watchlist features
  • Authenticate you and remember your signed-in session
  • Create and update private YouTube playlists at your request
  • Respond to feedback you send us
  • Protect the site from abuse and fix technical problems

We do not sell your personal information.

Third-party services

We rely on these services to run the site:

  • Google — Sign-in with Google and the YouTube Data API (playlist management only)
  • Supabase — Authentication and secure server-side storage for YouTube playlist credentials
  • Vercel — Site hosting
  • Resend — Delivering feedback emails to the site operator

Each provider processes data according to its own privacy policy. Google’s policies apply when you use Google sign-in or grant YouTube access.

Data retention

We keep your account and YouTube playlist credentials while your account is active and you use the YouTube queue feature. You can remove access at any time by signing out, revoking the app in your Google Account permissions, or contacting us (below). Feedback messages are kept only as long as needed to respond and maintain the site.

Your choices

  • Browse the fight list without signing in
  • Sign in only when you want YouTube queueing
  • Revoke Google or YouTube access at any time from your Google account settings
  • Clear watched-fight data stored in your browser using your browser’s site-data controls

Children

The site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do.
